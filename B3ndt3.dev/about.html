About Bendte Wehner


I'm glad you found your way here! As an author and former copy writer, it's surprisingly hard to write a text about myself. Who would have thought that?
Let's start with the fact that I graduated school with a focus on art and design.
After that, I first worked in retail, soon took care of merchandizing there and finally worked as a supervisor. Later I was office manager and then content manager.
I actually found my way into tech and my passion for frontend when I was already 30.
Fortunately, because here I feel like I've finally really arrived.

The good thing about that long way is that I can thus draw on a broad treasure trove of experience. And now that I have finished my vocational training, I am looking forward to gaining more of it.
